01 // BIO

Who the hell is A Rather Bright Light?

A Rather Bright Light is a St. Louis, MO-based indie rock and punk band that doesn't really care about the lines between genres. Equal parts singalong indie, urgent post-punk and basement-show energy, the band has been carving out their own corner of the scene since 2020.

Across three full-length records — Every Day Is Progress (2020), The Long Awaited (2022), and the new single "NO IDEA" (2026) — the group has sharpened a sound that's as comfortable on a packed club stage as it is on late-night headphones. Expect distorted hooks, gut-punch lyrics and a rhythm section that refuses to sit still.
